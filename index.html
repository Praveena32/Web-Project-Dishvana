Our Story

Rooted in Colombo,
Flavored with Passion

We started **Dishvana** with a simple dream: to bring premium, wood-fired-style culinary items to Colombo without the luxury price tag. We believe that everyone deserves high-quality dining, built on fresh ingredients and local integrity.

Combining classic baking techniques with the bold, fiery spices of Northern Sri Lanka, we create a menu that bridges worlds. From our devilled seafood toppings to aromatic local fried rice, we celebrate fusion tastes that bring friends and families together.
